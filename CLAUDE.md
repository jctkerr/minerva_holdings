# Minerva Holdings

## Business Context

AI-native legal managed services HoldCo. Non-regulated parent company that acquires and integrates legal operations businesses.

### Value Proposition

- **For corporate legal teams**: Scalable, AI-enabled operational support (privacy, cyber, IP, compliance)
- **For law firms**: Operational partner that extends capabilities without competingâ€”enables them to offer integrated advisory + operations
- **For insurers**: Structured incident response pathways

### Key Differentiator

Law-firm-aligned, not law-firm-competing. We strengthen law firms' client relationships, not disrupt them. Law firms provide legal advice and privilege; we provide the operational and technology engine.

### Business Units

1. **DataCo** (Initial)
   - DPO-as-a-Service (global)
   - Cyber incident response (24/7)
   - Digital forensics
   - Privacy operations

2. **IPCo** (Initial)
   - Trademark/patent docketing
   - IP portfolio management
   - Renewals management

3. **Future**
   - Regulatory compliance
   - Corporate formalities
   - Contract operations

### Target Clients

- Corporate legal departments (Fortune 500, mid-market, high-growth tech)
- PE portfolio companies
- Law firms (as channel partners)
- Cyber insurers
